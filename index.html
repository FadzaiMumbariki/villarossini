Our Story

A Legacy of
Italian Grandeur

Born in 1892 from the vision of Count Aldo Rossini, our villa has welcomed royalty, poets, and discerning travellers for over a century. Perched above the Val d'Orcia, every suite, garden, and candlelit table tells a story of passion, craftsmanship, and the deep warmth of Tuscan hospitality.

Experiences

Life at the Villa

Wine Cellar
Curated tastings in our 15th-century cellar with 4,000 bottles from the finest Italian estates.
Organic Spa
Traditional thermae rituals using locally sourced olive oil, volcanic clay and wildflower extracts.
Fine Dining
One Michelin-star Osteria Rossini — seasonal Tuscan cuisine by Executive Chef Marco Ferri.
Cycling Tours
Guided e-bike journeys through cypress-lined lanes and hilltop villages with picnic baskets.
Art Workshops
Fresco painting and ceramics in our garden atelier with Florentine master artisans.
Infinity Pool
A heated 25m infinity pool overlooking the Val d'Orcia, open year-round with poolside service.
Horse Riding
Morning rides through our private olive groves and truffle forests with expert equestrian guides.
Helicopter Transfer
Private helicopter arrivals from Florence, Pisa and Rome airports to our landscaped helipad.
